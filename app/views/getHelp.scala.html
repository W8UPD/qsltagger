Helping Us Help You.

QSLTagger is an open source project lead by Ricky Elrod (N8SQL), released under the Apache License version 2.

When things break, it can be frustrating, and we realize this. But in order to help us help you as best we can, we need some help from you.

When reporting an error, please include the following information.

  • The full URL that you experienced the bug on.
  • If relevant, the ID of the card you were tagging or viewing. This can be found in the title of the page.
  • Your browser, version, and operating system. Alternatively, the one-line result of going to this page.
  • Any other relevant information that you think would help.

Please do this. It will make our lives easier, and help us make your life easier.

This page might have been inspired by this relevant Phabricator article .

Once you've gotten that information...

...if you have a GitHub account, feel free to open an issue on the QSLTagger project.

Otherwise, you can email me directly at ricky.qsltagger@@elrod.me and I'll respond as soon as I can.
